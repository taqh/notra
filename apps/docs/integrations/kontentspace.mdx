Connect your Notra account to [Kontentspace](https://kontentspace.com/) via the Notra MCP and let your AI agent manage content directly.

## What you can do

Once connected, the Notra MCP enables your Kontentspace AI agent to:

- **Create posts** - Generate new content in your Notra workspace
- **Update posts** - Modify existing content and metadata
- **List posts** - Browse and search your content library
- **Manage content** - Organize and maintain your content pipeline

## Setup

1. **Create an API key** in your Notra workspace under the **API keys** section.
   - Choose between read-only or read-write access depending on what you want the AI agent to do.

2. **Open [Kontentspace connections](https://kontentspace.com/settings/connections)** and click **Connect** on the Notra MCP integration.

3. **Enter your API key** in the dialog and select the access level:
   - **Read-only** - The AI agent can view and search your content
   - **Read & Write** - The AI agent can also create and modify content

That's it - your Notra account is now connected to Kontentspace. You can start asking your AI agent to manage your Notra content.
