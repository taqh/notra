## Overview

Notra offers flexible pricing plans to match your team's needs. Manage your subscription, payment methods, and usage from the Billing page.

## Available Plans

Notra provides two main pricing tiers:

### Free Plan

**Perfect for hobbyists and individuals getting started.**

Includes:

* Limited content generation
* Basic integrations
* Limited team members
* Community support
* Standard features access

**Pricing:** \$0/month

### Pro Plan

**Designed for small teams with advanced needs.**

Includes:

* Increased content generation limits
* Priority AI processing
* More team members
* Advanced integrations
* Email support
* Custom brand voice
* API access
* Advanced automation features

**Pricing:**

* **Monthly:** Billed monthly
* **Yearly:** Billed annually (Save 20%)

<Info>
  Pro plans include a 3-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial period ends.
</Info>

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Current Plan

1. Navigate to **Billing** from your workspace sidebar
2. Your current plan is highlighted with a **Current** badge
3. View included features and usage limits

### Upgrading to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Billing">
    Go to **Billing** in your workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose billing period">
    Toggle between **Monthly** and **Yearly** billing. Yearly saves 20%.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start upgrade">
    Click **Upgrade to Pro** (or **Start Free Trial** if you're eligible).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    You'll be redirected to a secure checkout page to enter payment details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm subscription">
    Review the subscription details and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After upgrading, your new limits and features are available immediately.

### Downgrading to Free

<Warning>
  Downgrading may result in loss of access to Pro features and reduced usage limits. Content and data are preserved but may be read-only until within Free tier limits.
</Warning>

To downgrade:

1. Navigate to **Billing**
2. Click **Downgrade to Free** under the Free plan
3. Review the changes that will take effect
4. Confirm the downgrade

<Note>
  Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll retain Pro access until then.
</Note>

### Switching Between Monthly and Yearly

You can change your billing frequency:

1. Navigate to **Billing**
2. Click on your current Pro plan
3. Select **Update** or change to yearly/monthly
4. Confirm the change

Billing adjustments are prorated based on your current cycle.

## Payment Management

### Updating Payment Method

Payment details are managed through the secure checkout portal:

1. Navigate to **Billing**
2. Click on an invoice to open the hosted invoice page
3. Use the payment portal to update your card information

Alternatively, updating your subscription automatically prompts for new payment details.

### Payment Security

Notra uses industry-standard payment processing:

* All transactions are encrypted
* Payment details are securely stored by our payment provider
* We never store full credit card numbers
* PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure

## Invoices

View and download all your invoices from the Billing page.

### Viewing Invoices

1. Navigate to **Billing**
2. Click the **Billing** tab (if not already selected)
3. Scroll to the **Invoices** section

Each invoice displays:

* **Date** - When the invoice was created
* **Description** - What the charge was for (e.g., Pro Monthly, Pro Yearly)
* **Amount** - Total charged
* **Status** - Payment status (Paid, Pending, Failed)

### Downloading Invoices

Click on any invoice row to open the hosted invoice page where you can:

* View full invoice details
* Download a PDF copy
* See itemized charges
* Update payment method if needed

<Tip>
  Invoices are sorted by date. Click the date column header to sort ascending or descending.
</Tip>

## Usage Tracking

Monitor your feature usage to stay within plan limits and optimize your subscription.

### Viewing Usage

1. Navigate to **Billing**
2. Click the **Usage** tab
3. View current usage across all features

The Usage page shows:

* Feature name
* Current usage vs. limit
* Usage period (resets monthly)
* Progress bars for quick reference

### Usage Limits by Plan

Different features have different limits based on your plan:

**Content Generation:**

* Free: Limited AI-generated content pieces per month
* Pro: Higher limits for content generation

**Team Members:**

* Free: Fewer team member seats
* Pro: More team member seats

**Integrations:**

* Free: Basic integrations
* Pro: Advanced and custom integrations

**API Calls:**

* Free: Limited API access
* Pro: Higher rate limits

<Warning>
  When you reach a usage limit, the affected feature will be restricted until:

  * Your usage resets (monthly)
  * You upgrade to a higher plan
</Warning>

### Managing Usage

If you're approaching or have hit a limit:

1. **Upgrade your plan** - Get higher limits with Pro or contact sales for enterprise
2. **Wait for reset** - Usage limits reset at the start of each billing period
3. **Optimize usage** - Review your workflows to use features more efficiently

<Info>
  Usage tracking is updated in real-time, so you always know where you stand.
</Info>

## Billing FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the free trial work?">
    When you upgrade to Pro for the first time, you get a 3-day free trial. Your card is authorized but not charged. If you cancel during the trial, you won't be charged. After 3 days, your subscription begins and you'll be billed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes, you can downgrade or cancel your subscription at any time. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period, and you'll retain Pro access until then.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if my payment fails?">
    If a payment fails, we'll retry the charge and notify you via email. If payment continues to fail, your account may be downgraded to Free after a grace period. Update your payment method as soon as possible to maintain access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact support with your invoice details and reason for the refund request.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is usage calculated?">
    Usage is tracked based on the features you use during your billing period. Each feature has its own counter (e.g., content pieces generated, API calls made). All usage resets at the start of your new billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade mid-cycle?">
    Yes! When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Your new limits take effect immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I need more than Pro offers?">
    If your team needs higher limits, custom features, or dedicated support, contact our sales team for enterprise pricing and custom plans.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
