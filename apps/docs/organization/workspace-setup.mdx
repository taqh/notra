## Creating Your First Workspace

When you first sign up for Notra, you'll be prompted to create an organization workspace. This workspace is your team's home base where all content, automations, and integrations are managed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter organization details">
    Provide your organization name, a unique slug for URLs, and optionally your website URL. The slug will be used in all your workspace URLs: `app.usenotra.com/your-slug`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure workspace settings">
    After creation, customize your workspace logo, name, and URL slug from the **Settings > General** page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your brand identity">
    Navigate to **Brand > Identity** to configure your company profile and tone of voice for AI-generated content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Organization Settings

Manage your workspace settings from the **Settings > General** page.

### Basic Information

<ParamField path="Name" type="string" required>
  The display name of your organization as it appears across the platform. Must be between 2-100 characters.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Slug" type="string" required>
  A URL-friendly identifier used in workspace URLs. Must be 3-63 characters, lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only.

  <Warning>Reserved slugs like `api`, `auth`, `admin`, `settings`, and `dashboard` cannot be used.</Warning>
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Logo" type="image">
  Upload a custom logo for your organization. Accepted formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, AVIF.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Organization ID" type="string">
  A unique identifier for your organization. This is read-only and used for API integrations.
</ParamField>

### Updating Workspace Settings

1. Navigate to **Settings > General**
2. Click on the logo to upload a new image
3. Update the name or slug fields
4. Click **Save Changes** to apply

<Note>
  Changing your organization slug will update all workspace URLs. Make sure to update any bookmarks or links.
</Note>

## Brand Identity Configuration

Notra uses AI to generate content that matches your brand voice. Configure your brand settings to ensure all generated content is on-brand.

### Website Analysis

Notra can automatically extract your brand identity from your website:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your website URL">
    Go to **Brand > Identity** and enter your website URL (e.g., `example.com`)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run analysis">
    Click **Analyze** to start the automated brand extraction process. This typically takes 30-60 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review extracted information">
    Notra will extract your company name, description, and tone from your website content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Manual Configuration

You can also manually configure your brand identity:

#### Company Profile

<ParamField path="Company Name" type="string" required>
  Your official company or brand name.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Website" type="url">
  Your company website URL. Used for brand analysis and reference.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Description" type="string">
  A short overview of your company, what you do, and who you serve. This helps AI understand your business context.
</ParamField>

#### Tone & Language

Choose how AI should write on behalf of your brand:

**Tone Profile** (preset options):

* **Conversational** - Friendly, approachable, and casual
* **Professional** - Polished and business-appropriate
* **Casual** - Relaxed and informal
* **Formal** - Traditional and authoritative

**Custom Tone**:
Provide specific instructions about your brand voice (e.g., "Friendly but authoritative, use technical terms but explain them clearly")

<ParamField path="Custom Instructions" type="string">
  Add specific guidelines for AI-generated content, such as:

  * Phrases to avoid
  * Required terminology
  * Formatting preferences
  * Brand-specific requirements
</ParamField>

#### Target Audience

<ParamField path="Target Audience" type="string">
  Describe who you're writing for - their interests, pain points, expertise level, and what matters to them. This ensures content resonates with your readers.
</ParamField>

<Info>
  All brand settings auto-save as you edit. Changes apply immediately to new content generation.
</Info>

### Re-analyzing Your Brand

If your website or brand has changed, you can re-run the analysis:

1. Go to **Brand > Identity**
2. Click **Re-analyze** next to the Company Profile heading
3. Wait for the analysis to complete
4. Review and adjust any auto-populated fields

## Workspace Management

### Switching Between Workspaces

If you're a member of multiple organizations:

1. Click your organization name in the sidebar
2. Select a different organization from the dropdown
3. The workspace will switch automatically

Your last visited workspace is remembered for your next session.

### Deleting a Workspace

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace permanently removes all associated data including content, automations, integrations, and team members. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

To delete a workspace:

1. Navigate to **Settings > General**
2. Scroll to the **Danger Zone** section
3. Click **Delete Organization**
4. Confirm the deletion

**Requirements:**

* You must be the workspace owner
* You must have at least one other organization to switch to
* If other members exist, you'll be warned before deletion

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Choose a memorable slug** - Your organization slug appears in all workspace URLs. Pick something short, recognizable, and related to your brand.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Complete your brand profile** - The more detailed your brand identity, the better AI-generated content will match your voice and style.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Update brand settings regularly** - As your brand evolves, keep your Notra settings current by re-running website analysis or updating manual fields.
</Tip>
